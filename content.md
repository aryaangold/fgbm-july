# BEFORE WE BEGIN: HOW THE YOUTUBE CONTENT IS USED  

---  

The 40 insights from the 2025 Makurdi Regional Convention are gold. Here is how they are deployed across July:  

**Usage Strategy:**  
- Insights become **quote graphics** — designed cards with the teaching point  
- Insights become **TikTok/Reels hooks** — "Here's something FGBMFI taught me about business..."  
- Insights become **caption themes** — the body of social media posts built around each teaching  
- Insights become **carousel content** — multi-slide educational posts  
- Insights establish **credibility and depth** — showing that FGBMFI conventions produce real, substantive content (not just hype)  
- They are used to **answer the younger audience question:** "Why should I attend this?"  

**The framing:**  
*"This is what happened at one of our regional conventions last year. Imagine what the National Convention in Lagos is going to produce."*
